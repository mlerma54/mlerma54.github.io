Special Assignment

The purpose of this assignment is to collect ideas intended to improve various aspects of CS 310.

Assume that you are a professor in a new university that has just been created. You are assigned the task of designing and organizing a new course with title "Mathematical Foundations of Computer Science." The course will be required for Computer Science majors and for some other courses in Engineering and other careers. Its content should roughly consists of selected subjects in Discrete Mathematics plus some applications to Computer Science. You need to decide (justifying your decision if possible) things like:

  • Syllabus for the course. You may use the index of any book on Discrete Mathematics as a guide for the material to be covered.

  • Time that should be spent on it, say 1 quarter, 2 quarters, a whole year... Assume that there are 3 lectures per week plus perhaps 1 or 2 problem/quiz sessions.

  • Logistics: number of midterms, problem sessions, quizzes, grading policy...

  • Textbook and other teaching materials.

  • Teaching resources, say, 1 instructor, 1 TA, 1 grader for homework (per section)?

  • Whatever other aspects of the course you may consider relevant, such a the size of the class, etc.

Send me your thoughts by email or using the "other comments" field in the feedback feature. You do not need to think all the items, any idea abut any of them may also be useful. Also any ideas about how to improve the current CS 310 would also be appreciated.
